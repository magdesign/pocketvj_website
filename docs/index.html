How do I manage content ?

You can access the PocketVJ via Wifi and manage everything
with your webbrowser. It works with all operating systems.

Multi-channel video installation ?

Yes, you are able to run an unlimited number of players in sync.

Can I sync videomapping ?

Multi-channel synced videomapping is possible on the Exhibition.

What about dmx?

On the Exhibition you can record and playback dmx/artnet.
It is possible to playback a light show together with audio or video.

Can I start videos with my light console ?

Yes, the Exhibition can be controlled with artnet/dmx/osc/udp.

Is NDI supported ?

No, use Syphon or VNC instead.
